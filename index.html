Stop Guessing What to Test

Turn Jira tickets into test strategy, automation plans, and release decisions in seconds — not hours.

The Problem

QA engineers spend hours analyzing Jira tickets:

  • • What should we test?
  • • What are the edge cases?
  • • Should this be automated?

This process is slow, repetitive, and inconsistent.

The Solution

Vooabi turns every Jira ticket into structured QA intelligence:

  • ✔ Test scenarios generated automatically
  • ✔ Automation strategy (UI vs API)
  • ✔ Risk score (0–100)
  • ✔ Release decision (GO / CAUTION / BLOCK)

Why It Matters

Save hours of manual QA analysis and reduce release risk — instantly.
